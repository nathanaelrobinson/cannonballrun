Sign up with your friends to race other Runners Across the Country

What is the Cannonball Run

The Cannonball Run is a virtual relay race from New York City to Los Angeles.

  • Create a team or sign up to join one!
  • Your Strava runs will get logged towards your team's relay total.
  • Track your progress against others on the map and be the first to reach Los Angeles

Teams will be starting races every week. Feel free to join multiple teams
